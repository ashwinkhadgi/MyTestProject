Last updated: August 11, 2025

1.1 Images You Provide

ChartAI processes chart images that you voluntarily upload to analyze and extract insights. These images are processed locally on your device and/or sent to our servers for analysis purposes only.

We may collect certain information automatically when you use our app:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Crash logs and error reports to improve app stability
  • Anonymous analytics data to understand user behavior

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number) unless voluntarily provided
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or other personal data from your device
  • Financial or sensitive personal information
